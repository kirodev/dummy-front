Mobile communication

Mobile communication technology permits mobile handset users to connect with others from different locations..

Connected Vehicles

A connected vehicle allows drivers to access bi-directional communication between vehicles, handsets, and infrastructures.

Internet of Things

A network technology that involves physical devices with sensors to collect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet.

Audiovisual electronics

Media technology uses codecs, such as MP3 for audio and H.264 for video, for efficient compression and decompression of audio and video data, enabling high-quality content storage, streaming, and playback.
