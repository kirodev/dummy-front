Getting Started

To access the platform, log in using your account credentials. If you don't have an account, click Sign Up on the login page and follow the prompts.

Platform Features

  • Dashboard: View key metrics and performance insights.
  • Reports: Generate detailed reports on demand.
  • Collaboration: Share and manage projects with team members.
  • Notifications: Stay informed with real-time alerts.

Navigation

The platform interface is divided into the following areas:

  • Header: Access account settings, notifications, and help.
  • Sidebar: Navigate to different modules such as Dashboard, Reports, and Settings.
  • Main Area: View and interact with data or tools related to the selected module.

Core Processes

1. Creating a New Project

Follow these steps to create a project:

  1. Go to the Projects module from the sidebar.
  2. Click the Create New Project button.
  3. Fill in the project details and click Save.

2. Generating Reports

To generate a report:

  1. Navigate to the Reports section.
  2. Select the desired parameters and click Generate.
  3. Download or share the report as needed.

FAQ

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Click Forgot Password on the login page and follow the instructions.

Q: Can I collaborate with external users?

A: Yes, you can invite external users via email to specific projects.
